# KQURADA_CASH
KQURADA_CASH is web application that helps you track and record all the sales and expenses of your business on the go just by scanning the kqurada_cash booklet and at the same time marketing your products/services to the world.

The app also has other nice feature for checking daly accurate weather forecast in all the world cities, calculator, currency conversion and business financials calculator for calculating loans, savings returns, mortgage, future value ete...
